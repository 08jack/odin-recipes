Toast

Description

Vegemite on toast. You can't go wrong with this old australian classic.

You can subsitute the Vegemite with Marmite if you need.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread.
  • Butter.
  • Vegemite.

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread in toaster.
  2. Prepare plate, butter and vegemite.
  3. Once bread is toasted, place on plate.
  4. Butter the bread.
  5. Apply Vegemite and enjoy!
