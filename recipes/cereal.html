Cereal

Description

Good old cereal! Perfect for breakfast, and great for when you can't be bothered making anything else for lunch.

The brand that I like the most is cornflakes. They taste alright and they're not terribly unhealthy either.

Ingredients

  • Milk.
  • A cereal of your choosing.

Instructions

  1. Add cereal to a bowl, make sure to leave enough room for milk.
  2. Pour milk into the bowl.
  3. Enjoy your meal!
